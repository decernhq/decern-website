# Changelog

## 2026-04-10 — Marketing copy repositioning

### Why
Repositioned Decern's messaging around a primary buyer (Platform Engineering / DevEx leaders at 30–150 dev orgs adopting AI coding tools). Previous copy addressed three audiences equally, missed the compliance angle, and communicated "side project" pricing.

### What changed

**Homepage**
- Hero: added buyer-targeting line for platform teams using Copilot/Cursor/Claude Code
- "Sound familiar?" section promoted to directly after hero (was mid-page)
- Pain bullets rewritten punchier with specifics (800-line diff, review fatigue, 10x speed)
- Added closing line: "This isn't a review problem. It's a policy problem."
- New "Audit-ready by design" section with compliance badges (EU AI Act, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, SOC 2)
- "Who it's for" reordered: Platform/DevEx first (was third), copy strengthened
- New "Deploy in your own VPC" self-hosted callout section
- Social proof placeholder section (hidden, ready for partner logos)
- Terminology broadened: "architecture decisions and engineering rules" throughout

**Pricing**
- Switched from flat monthly to per-developer pricing
- Free: €0, up to 5 devs, observation only
- Team: €12/dev/month, min 5 devs, blocking mode
- Business: €28/dev/month, min 15 devs, SSO + audit log
- Enterprise/Self-Hosted: from €2,500/month, custom
- Added developer count FAQ note
- Updated CTAs to "Start Team/Business trial" and "Contact sales"

**i18n**
- All changes applied to both en.json and it.json
